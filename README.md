# Delete Account – PDF to Excel Converter

Last updated: 2026

PDF to Excel Converter allows users to request deletion of their account and associated data.

## How to Request Account Deletion

If you would like to delete your account and associated data, please follow these steps:

1. Open the PDF to Excel Converter app.
2. Go to **Account Settings**.
3. Select **Delete Account** and confirm your request.

Alternatively, you can request account deletion by contacting us via email.

## What Data is Deleted

When your account is deleted, the following data will be permanently removed:

* Account profile information
* Uploaded files
* Conversion history

## Data Retention

Some data may be retained temporarily for security, legal, or fraud-prevention purposes. Such data will be deleted within **90 days**.
